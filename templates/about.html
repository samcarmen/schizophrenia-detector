Schizophrenia Detector

Schizophrenia is a serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally. According to MentalHelp.net , Schizophreania alone has affected about 1% of the entire human population this year.

The purpose of this project is to create an automated system to perform binary classification between healthy and schizophrenic patients. On this web application that we developed, users are able to upload EEG data and receive a prediction of whether the subject is of schizophrenic or not.

This system could potentially be beneficial to patients who are suffering from schizophrenia and it could reduce the time and cost on diagnosis of the disease. The users are able to see the visualisation of the feature extracted data and the graph generated for the signals as well.
